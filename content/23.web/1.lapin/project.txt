Gigantic, scrollable and zoomable digital collage. Assembled from a hundred digital scans.

-   <strong>Skills:</strong>
Make the collage available online despite its size, develop a workflow able to deal with humongous files (30,000px*17,000px), find methods to assemble dozens of scanned images

-   <strong>Tools:</strong>
Portable scanner, Photoshop CS6, Google Map API, HTML, Javascript, ImageMagick, bash, ink, paper
